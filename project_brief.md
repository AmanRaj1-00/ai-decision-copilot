 AI Decision Copilot

 Problem
Managers receive large volumes of business data but lack clear, prioritized, and explainable actions.

 Solution
An AI-driven decision intelligence platform that:
- Detects business signals
- Forecasts future impact
- Recommends ranked managerial actions
- Explains reasoning with numeric evidence
- Provides confidence score

 Target User
Mid-level retail/operations manager.

 Domain (Initial Scope)
Retail sales & inventory only.

 Inputs
- Sales data
- Inventory data

 Outputs
- Ranked action list
- Expected impact
- Confidence score
- Short rationale

 Success Metrics
- Logical correctness of decisions
- Forecast accuracy
- Explainability completeness
- Demo usability

 Deliverables
- Working Streamlit demo
- FastAPI backend
- GitHub repo
- 2–3 minute demo video
